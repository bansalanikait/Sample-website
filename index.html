How to Join SQC?


    
Who can join?

    Any student of NIT Jalandhar with interest in robotics,
    electronics, AI, or problem solving.


    
Do I need prior experience?

    No. We train members from basics to advanced systems.


    
What do members gain?

    Hands-on projects, competition exposure, teamwork,
    and strong technical foundations.


    
How are selections done?

    Through basic interviews, tasks, and enthusiasm to learn.
